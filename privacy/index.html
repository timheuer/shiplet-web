Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Shiplet ("we," "our," or "the app") is a package tracking application developed by Tim Heuer. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Shiplet.

Summary

  • We don't sell your data — Your information is never sold to third parties.
  • Minimal data collection — We only collect what's necessary to track your packages.
  • Your data stays yours — All data is stored on your device and your private iCloud account.
  • No tracking or analytics — We don't use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use Shiplet, you may provide the following information:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Package tracking numbers To retrieve delivery status from carriers Device & iCloud
Package names To help you identify your packages Device & iCloud
Tags To organize your packages Device & iCloud

Information Collected Automatically

Data Type Purpose Storage
Tracking status Retrieved from carrier APIs (USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, OnTrac) based on your tracking numbers Device & iCloud
Package location Approximate location data provided by carriers during transit Device & iCloud

Camera Access

Shiplet requests camera access only to scan package tracking barcodes. Camera data is:

  • Processed entirely on-device
  • Never recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • Used solely to extract tracking number text

You can deny camera access and manually enter tracking numbers instead.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  1. Display package tracking status — We query carrier APIs with your tracking numbers to show delivery updates.
  2. Sync across your devices — If you enable iCloud, your packages sync privately through your Apple iCloud account.
  3. Provide offline access — Package data is cached locally so you can view it without an internet connection.

We do not use your information for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • User profiling or analytics
  • Sale to third parties
  • Any purpose other than tracking your packages

Data Storage & Security

On-Device Storage

All package data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework with standard iOS data protection.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled for Shiplet:

  • Your data syncs through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Only you can access your data through your Apple ID
  • We cannot access your iCloud data

You can disable iCloud sync for Shiplet in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud.

Carrier API Communication

When refreshing tracking information:

  • Your tracking numbers are sent to carrier APIs (USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, OnTrac) via EasyPost, a third-party tracking service that acts as an intermediary
  • EasyPost temporarily stores tracking numbers to facilitate communication with carrier services
  • EasyPost may also be used as a fallback tracking service when direct carrier APIs are unavailable
  • Communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • Carrier and EasyPost privacy policies apply to data they process

Third-Party Services

Shipping Carriers

Shiplet communicates with these carrier services:

When you add a tracking number, that number is shared with the respective carrier to retrieve status. Carriers may log API requests per their policies.

Shiplet does not guarantee carrier availability or delivery accuracy and is dependent on third-party shipping services.

EasyPost Tracking Service

Shiplet uses EasyPost as an intermediary service to facilitate communication with carrier APIs:

  • Purpose: EasyPost acts as a unified API gateway to retrieve tracking information from multiple carriers
  • Data Shared: Tracking numbers are temporarily stored by EasyPost to query carrier systems
  • Fallback Service: EasyPost may also serve as an alternative tracking data source when direct carrier APIs are unavailable
  • Privacy Policy: easypost.com/privacy

Apple Services

  • iCloud/CloudKit — For optional cross-device sync (Apple Privacy)
  • MapKit — For displaying package location (no personal data shared)

Data Retention

  • Package data is retained until you delete it from the app
  • Archived packages remain until you permanently delete them
  • Deleted data is removed from your device and iCloud (sync may take time)

To delete all your data:

  1. Delete individual packages via swipe actions
  2. Or delete the app (removes all local data)
  3. iCloud data can be managed in iOS Settings

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — View all data stored in the app at any time
  • Delete — Remove any or all packages and their history
  • Portability — Your data syncs via iCloud and can be accessed on any of your devices
  • Opt-out of iCloud — Use the app with local-only storage

Children's Privacy

Shiplet does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences tracking their own package deliveries.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

California Residents (CCPA)

Shiplet does not sell personal information. California residents have the right to know what data is collected (detailed above) and request deletion at any time through the app.

European Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area:

  • Legal basis: Legitimate interest in providing the service you requested
  • Data controller: Tim Heuer
  • No cross-border transfers: Data stays on your device and Apple's iCloud infrastructure
